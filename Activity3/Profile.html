Ahyeon (아현)

Stage Name: Ahyeon (아현)

Birth Name: Jung Ahyeon (정아현)

Chinese Name: Zhèng Yǎ Xuān (郑雅譞)

Position: Vocalist, Rapper, Dancer, Center (?)

Birthday: April 11, 2007

Zodiac Sign: Aries

Nationality: Korean

Profile

Jung Ah Yeon (정아현; born Apr 11, 2007), simply known as Ahyeon, is a South Korean member of the K-pop group BABYMONSTER under YG Entertainment. She made her debut in 2023 at the age of 15.


Fun facts and trivia

Ahyeon was 15 years old when she was revealed as a new member of BABYMONSTER in 2023.
Jung Ah Yeon was a student at Hanlim Multi Arts School and Daeryong Middle School.
Ahyeon's position in BABYMONSTER is of a rapper and vocalist. She is considered an all-rounder who can sing, rap, and dance.
She is Korean and has a younger brother.
She passed the first audition for Banana Culture Entertainment in 2017 and the YG audition in 2018.
She trained for 4 years.
Ahyeon can speak Korean, English, and Chinese fluently. She started learning Chinese at the age of 7 and English at the age of 5.
She was the second member of BABYMONSTER revealed.


Career Highlights

Jung Ahyeon was born on April 11, 2007, in Gangwon-do, South Korea. She joined the Ara Dance Academy in 2017 at the age of 11. She then passed the first round of auditions with Banana Culture Entertainment. She applied to enter the 2018 survival show, Produce 48. In February 2018, she passed the auditions with YG Entertainment and was a trainee for 4 years prior to her debut with BABYMONSTER.


Family

Ahyeon's family is comprised of her parents and a younger brother.

Riracha Phondechaphiphat (born Feb 17, 2009), better known as Chiquita, is a Thai K-pop idol. She is a member of the K-pop group BABYMONSTER under YG Entertainment. Chiquita made her debut in 2023.


Fun facts and trivia

Chiquita was 14 years old when she was revealed as a member of BABY MONSTER.
She is the youngest member (maknae) of BABYMONSTER.
She was the 3rd member of BABYMONSTER revealed.
Chiquita was the first K-pop member from a girl group born in 2009 to debut in the industry.
She has an older brother named Copper, who is a 789 trainee.
She trained for over 2 years.


Career Highlights

Born in 2009, Chiquita became the youngest member to debut in YG Entertainment as of 2023. She trained for 2 years before finally debuting with BABYMONSTER.


Family

Chiquita was born on February 17, 2009 in Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand. Her family is composed of her mother (born in 1981), her father, and an older brother (born in 2006). Her brother, named Copper, is a 789 trainee.

Kawai Ruka (河井瑠花; born Mar 20, 2002), simply known as Ruka, is a Japanese dancer, rapper, and a member of the K-pop group BABYMONSTER under YG Entertainment. She made her debut in 2023 at the age of 20.

Ruka was born in Japan on Mar 20, 2002, as an only child. In 2018, at 16, she passed the audition with YG Entertainment. During training, she was praised for her strong dancing skills, which she had been mastering for 10 years. She trained for 4-5 years before her debut with BABYMONSTER.
